Welcome to SoIT

The School of Information Technology caters the highest percentage of the student population. Its separation from the Department of Engineering and Technology was approved by the BOT on May 13, 2008. As a separate department, it is manned by seven regular instructors, two temporary status and two contract of service instructors back then. Headed by their Dean, Dr. Mayreen V. Amazona, it continues to provide quality trainings needed by the students.

Milestones

HYPER INTERDISCIPLINARY CONFERENCE IN THE PHILIPPINES 2023

On November 11, 2023, the School of Information Technology, along with universities across the country, witnessed a convergence of brilliant minds and groundbreaking ideas at the Hyper Interdisciplinary Conference in the Philippines 2023. The event served as a dynamic platform for professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts to delve into the transformative realm of reformative technologies. From artificial intelligence to sustainable energy solutions, the conference sparked insightful discussions on how these innovations can be tailored for localized impact, addressing unique challenges within the Philippine context.
